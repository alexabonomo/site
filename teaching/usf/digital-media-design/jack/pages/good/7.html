On the miles stretch of lines and lines and lines, you feel a bump.

Out in the vastness of the darkness, you hit something infinitesimally small.

The humming fades, the asynchronous creaking of rubber as you and your boots slide towards the speck.



A dead cat lays before you.

Bathed in its old blood. As if it’s baptized by it.

Curled with paws raw, the fur wet with last night’s deluge of sludge.

Maroon wakes of innerads rains from the orifices.

This is nothing like you have seen before.

The two-ton tanks often squash critters into fine fur rugs.

But it didn’t grace this poor soul the same fate.

Rather, you see the threaded treads indented in its skin.

An adobe brick mural smashed into shattered fragile form.



A beautiful fallen angel.

Ripped from the heavens, as the lights fades from her eyes.

Never to feel the tender embrace again.

Feel the warmth of an open fire. Only darkness awaits.

She lies motionless…..
